## Sprint log

### Sprint 1 — week of 2026-03-23
**Idea:** Direct corporate AI training (no intermediary)

**Hypothesis:** Companies that have consumed AI training through BCG/CuriousCore would engage me directly at lower cost if they knew I was the actual trainer — saving 50–70% vs platform rates while getting better customisation.

**Why this first:** Fastest path to validating the Fracxional consulting-client goal (due May). Uses warm network with zero cold outreach. Result directly feeds or kills the offer.md one-pager.

**Experiment:**
1. Identify 5 warm contacts: past training participants, L&D managers at JERA/BCG client orgs, or colleagues who've referred training work
2. Send a personalised note (not a pitch — a "hey I'm now taking direct clients" message) to each
3. Offer a 30-min discovery call
4. Track: opens, replies, calls booked

**Score criteria:**
- 0–1: Kill. Wrong channel or wrong offer
- 2: Pivot. Audience is right but message/offer needs rework
- 3+: Escalate. Send proposals, develop offer further

**Deadline:** Results assessed by 2026-03-30 (before IBKR/CPF deadline week)
