## Goal
Build working fluency in product, growth, and marketing by Sep 2026 — enough to run discovery, write strategy, and credibly consult on it at portfolio companies and through Fracxional.

## Why this matters
Currently strong in engineering and data. Weak in product/growth/marketing means: (1) limited consulting surface area for Fracxional, (2) unable to do credible GTM/growth DD at Iterative, (3) harder to evaluate product-market fit in portfolio companies.

---

## Month 1–2: Product foundations (Apr–May)
- [ ] Read *Continuous Discovery Habits* (Teresa Torres) — apply opportunity-solution tree to one Fracxional client problem
- [ ] Read *Inspired* (Marty Cagan) — extract 5 key principles for early-stage B2B SaaS
- [ ] Write 1 PRD from scratch for an internal tool or workshop product
- [ ] Learn and apply a prioritisation framework (RICE or ICE) to an actual backlog
- [ ] Shadow or debrief 2 portfolio company product leads on their discovery process (leverage Iterative access)

## Month 2–3: Growth fundamentals (May–Jun)
- [ ] Complete *Reforge Growth Series* or equivalent (Lenny's Newsletter archive is free) — focus on growth loops, acquisition, retention
- [ ] Map a growth model (acquisition → activation → retention → referral → revenue) for one real product (Fracxional or portfolio co)
- [ ] Study 3 SEA startup growth case studies (can pull from Iterative portfolio — Intellect, Botsync, etc.)
- [ ] Understand PLG vs. sales-led vs. marketing-led: write a 1-page comparison with when to use each
- [ ] Build a basic north-star metric + 3 supporting metrics for a product you know well

## Month 3–4: Marketing fundamentals (Jun–Jul)
- [ ] Study positioning and messaging: read *Obviously Awesome* (April Dunford) — apply to Fracxional
- [ ] Write a positioning one-pager for Fracxional (ICP, problem, differentiation, proof points)
- [ ] Understand content marketing vs. demand gen vs. brand — know when each applies
- [ ] Publish 3 technical/thought leadership pieces (feeds Fracxional pipeline → [[scale-side-income]])
- [ ] Study 2 B2B SaaS GTM motions end-to-end (e.g. how Notion, Linear, or Retool went from 0 to first 100 enterprise clients)

## Month 5–6: Integration and application (Aug–Sep)
- [ ] Do a mock product/growth/marketing audit for one Iterative portfolio company — write up findings
- [ ] Deliver 1 product or growth workshop via Fracxional or a teaching institution
- [ ] Build a "product × growth × marketing" consulting framework doc usable as a client deliverable
- [ ] Assess: can you now run a 2-hour discovery workshop with a non-technical client? If not, identify gap and close it
