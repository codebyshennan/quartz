## Goal
Achieve three calisthenics movements: planche, handstand pushups, and L-sit.

## Key results
### L-sit (easiest — start here)
- [ ] Floor L-sit support hold: 10 seconds
- [ ] Parallel bar L-sit: 5 seconds
- [ ] Parallel bar L-sit: 15 seconds
- [ ] Full L-sit hold: 10 seconds

### Handstand pushups
- [ ] Pike pushups: 3×10
- [ ] Deficit pike pushups: 3×8
- [ ] Wall-supported HSPU: 5 reps
- [ ] Wall-supported HSPU: 10 reps
- [ ] Strict HSPU (freestanding or wall): 3 reps

### Planche (hardest — long-term)
- [ ] Planche lean: 10 seconds
- [ ] Tuck planche: 5 seconds
- [ ] Tuck planche: 15 seconds
- [ ] Advanced tuck planche: 5 seconds
- [ ] Straddle planche: 3 seconds
- [ ] Full planche: 3 seconds
